Leadership

Pastor Paul Nam, founder and Lead Pastor of Grace Community Chapel Sydney, is a graduate of The George Washington University (B.A.) and The Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary (M.Div). Currently pursuing a Doctorate of Ministry in Discipleship from Talbot School of Theology, he also holds a Foundations of Biblical Counseling Certificate from Christian Counseling & Educational Foundation (CCEF) and is ordained in the Christian Reformed Church of North America (CRCNA). His passion lies in raising bold, committed, and passionate disciples of Jesus Christ for a significant impact in the Kingdom of God. Pastor Paul desires people to cultivate a deep, love relationship with Jesus. A devoted husband to Deborah and a grateful father to Karis, Kayla, Moses, and Matthew.
